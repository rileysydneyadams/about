My Story

Where the love for aesthetics, photography, and storytelling began.

Early Life

I was born on January 9, 1999, in Santa Barbara, California. I grew up in a family that valued creativity and independence. My father worked as an architect specializing in residential design, while my mother taught literature at a local high school.

From an early age, I developed an appreciation for aesthetics, photography, and storytelling. Weekend road trips along the California coast and family vacations to national parks played a major role in shaping my love for travel and visual experiences.

As a child, I spent countless hours collecting magazines, creating mood boards, and decorating journals with photographs and handwritten notes. Friends and family often noticed my natural eye for colors, fashion, and design.

Education

I attended Santa Barbara High School from 2013 to 2017, where I was involved in the photography club and helped organize school events.

In 2018, I moved to Los Angeles to pursue higher education and enrolled at the University of Southern California, earning a Bachelor of Arts in Communication with a Minor in Visual Design.

From 2018 to 2022, I took part in creative projects, student media activities, and several internships in digital marketing and branding — where I became fascinated with the intersection of storytelling, photography, and online culture.

My Journey

From a creative agency desk to building a life around content I love making.

Early Career

After graduating from USC in 2022, I joined a boutique creative agency in Los Angeles, handling social media management, brand strategy, content planning, visual campaigns, and influencer collaborations. I enjoyed the work, but gradually realized I preferred creating rather than working behind the scenes.

Turning Point

Around 2022, I began documenting my daily life, fashion inspirations, favorite coffee shops, weekend trips, and apartment décor. A series called "California Weekend Diaries" unexpectedly gained attention, and instead of chasing trends I focused on authenticity, minimalism, and quality storytelling. By 2024, brands started reaching out, and by 2025 I had transitioned into full-time content creation and freelance consulting.
